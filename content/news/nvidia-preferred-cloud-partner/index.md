We are pleased to announce that Freyr Technology AI Pte. Ltd. has officially
renewed its NVIDIA Preferred Cloud Partner – Compute competency status within
the NVIDIA Partner Network (NPN) program, effective April 15, 2026.

## Scalable compute for the next generation of AI

This renewed status reflects Freyr AI’s continued focus on dependable,
high-performance infrastructure for AI builders and enterprises around the
world.
